Hello there!

I am Senior undergraduate Computer and Systems Engineer at Universidad de Los Andes, Bogota, Colombia. Applying to Ph.D for Fall 2020.
My main interests lie on creating accessible technologies, creating systems that provide accessible content and innovating the mediums of interaction that visually impaired people use.

I have been very lucky to be mentored by multiple Professors in research and Accessibility, to mention some: John Guerra-Gomez, who introduced me to this wonderful world of creating Accessible solutions that have real-life impact on people daily life and Shiri Azenkot, who has further developed my interests working at the Enhancing Ability Lab and introducing me to her first PhD student, Lei Shi. Lei has been a friend, an advisor, a mentor and an example to follow. He has helped me a lot with my research work and my career goals. I am really grateful to Shiri for introducing him to me.

At this moment, I continue to work under Shiri's supervision in the Enhancing Ability Lab on Talkit and Markit (research) which involves accessibility, human-computer interaction, and augmented reality.
